# Cheerfall -- Brand Guidelines

Version 1.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Brand Core

## Brand Name

**Cheerfall**

## Brand Essence

*Joy is contagious. Spread it.*

## Mission

To create the world's most uplifting digital space by publishing
positive global news and empowering users to share their personal wins.

## Vision

To become the go-to destination for emotional uplift in a noisy digital
world.

## Positioning

Cheerfall is emotionally intelligent media designed to counter
negativity bias without becoming naive or unrealistic.\
Tone: Grounded optimism.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Brand Personality

Primary Archetype: The Optimist\
Secondary Archetype: The Community Builder

Traits: - Encouraging but not cheesy\
- Warm but not childish\
- Confident but not preachy\
- Calm but energetic

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Tone & Voice

## Writing Guidelines

**Do:** - Use clear, short sentences\
- Highlight human impact\
- Use active voice\
- Focus on outcomes and emotion

**Do Not:** - Use exaggerated hype language\
- Overuse emojis\
- Sound like motivational spam\
- Use toxic positivity phrases

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Visual Identity

## Brand Colors

Primary Gradient: - Warm Sunrise Orange: `#FF8A00` - Soft Coral:
`#FF4E50`

Secondary: - Sky Blue: `#4FC3F7` - Mint Green: `#34D399`

Neutrals: - Background: `#F9FAFB` - Dark Text: `#1F2937` - Light Text:
`#6B7280` - White: `#FFFFFF`

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Typography

Primary Font: Inter\
Fallback: System UI, sans-serif

Font Weights: - 700: Headlines\
- 600: Section Titles\
- 400: Body\
- 300: Captions

Font Scale: - H1: 48px\
- H2: 36px\
- H3: 24px\
- Body: 16--18px\
- Caption: 14px

Line Height: 1.6

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. UI/UX Principles

Design must feel: - Airy\
- Light\
- Emotionally calming

Principle:\
**Breathing space is emotional space.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Layout Structure

Homepage Sections: 1. Hero Section (Headline + Subheadline + CTA)\
2. Trending Positive News\
3. Community Wins\
4. Global Stories Map (Optional)\
5. Submit Story CTA

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Content Categories

## Global News

-   Innovation\
-   Community Impact\

-   Environment Wins\
-   Education\
-   Youth Achievements

## User Stories

-   Career Wins\
-   Personal Growth\
-   Family Moments\
-   Fitness Milestones\
-   Recovery Stories\
-   Small Daily Wins

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Moderation Philosophy

Allowed: - Real struggles with positive outcomes\
- Honest vulnerability\
- Authentic storytelling

Not Allowed: - Political propaganda\
- Hate speech\
- Misinformation\
- Trauma exploitation\
- Toxic positivity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Emotional KPI Framework

Primary Metrics: - Time spent per story\
- Saves\
- Shares\
- Repeat visits\
- User submissions

Focus on emotional retention, not just clicks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Brand Guardrails

Cheerfall must never become: - Gossip media\
- Political commentary hub\
- Clickbait machine\
- Meme spam page

If engagement drops, improve storytelling quality --- not
sensationalism.
